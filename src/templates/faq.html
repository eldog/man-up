Frequently Asked Questions

MAN UP?

Yeah, that's right.

Why?

Because for too long have us nerds stood by, complacent with our efforts. It's high time we took it to the limit. Utilise and maximise our skills.

How?

Through a society where diligence and enthusiasm are rewarded. Through a society where one can achieve their nerdiest dreams. Through a society where potential is noticed and embraced.

Err...

Okay enough with the grandiose speeches. Basically we want to make cool things - with code.

Cool code?

We want people to start noticing the computer scientists here at Manchester and why shouldn't they? We're the ones on campus with the knowledge of today's computer systems and should be able to demonstrate the 'creative' and 'interesting' things we can do with them.

So is it like a Q&A session for my java labs?

No.

So you're not going to actually help with my studies?

No and even more importantly that is not the point of the society. We aim to help develop the skills you can't learn in the class room. The skills you need to actually create something cool in computer science. The skill of having a go and doing a task - no matter how insanely impossible it seems.

Sounds like a load of effort...

It is and if putting a load of effort into something that has no immediate gratifying reward isn't your cup of earl gray then MAN-UP is probably not for you.

Actually, I do think I could see myself spending night after night tackling a problem that does not necessarily have a solution.
