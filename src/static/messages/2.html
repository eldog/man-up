Hello all,

This Saturday is the first Man-UP Hack-a-thon. Anyone can enter, either by yourself or as a team. To enter, come to this week's meeting, Thursday 5pm in LF15.

In the hack-a-thon, teams will work together, throughout the day, to create pub games that will played by groups of judges at Student Union bar in the evening. The winning team will receive the coveted reality mug.

In order to prep everyone, we'll be hosting a Hack-a-thon training Masterclass at 2pm tomorrow (Wednesday 23rd February) in LF15, Kilburn Building. We will be going over tips, tricks and tools that will be useful in the Hack-a-thon.

There will also be the usual meeting on Thursday at 5pm in LF15, where we've got some talks by fellow students and where we will also be registering teams for the Hack-a-thon and making sure every one knows what to expect on the day.

Happy hacking,

Lloyd
