Want to work for a great company like Facebook, Spotify, Apple or Google?

Want to do something more interesting than developing Java Enterprise Systems for the rest of your life?

Then get some skills and come to Manchester Ultimate Programming’s first 24 hour hack-a-thon this Saturday 26th March, LF15, Kilburn Building.

The goal is to create a StarCraft AI, defeat your opponents, rise through the tournament and become the ultimate programming champion.

Full details at to today's meeting, 5pm in LF15.

It will be using the technology used for the AIIDE Starcraft Competition

However, we will be just focused on creating bots for the first tournament

Getting started

No previous experience required.

This competition is suitable for all years, 1st, 2nd, 3rd and beyond. Worried you won’t be able to do it? In 50 lines of code you beat the built-in AI. You don't need to know anything about Windows development, C++ or even StarCraft; we've prepared a starter kit and we will be on hand to help throughout the event. You don't even need Windows.

Employers care about what you've done, not the exam results you got. Vastly improve your employment prospects: create something you can talk about and do something outside of your course that is actually interesting. It's only one day in your whole life.
