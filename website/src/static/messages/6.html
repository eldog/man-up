Hi all,

What: Hack evening

When: Thursday 14th April 5pm - 7pm LF15, Kilburn Building [Out of hours pass required past 6pm]

Man-UP is preparing for a big launch next year. In preparation we're overhauling the website. We're starting with better ways to register and communicate with members.

As a tech society, our plan is to use a card reader to automatically register members from their student cards, this will be both useful (less effort) and awesome (computer magic).

We have a number of tasks for you to hack at;

  • Card reading software
  • e-mail verification on Google Appengine
  • Mapping swipe cards to members

Come to the meeting, pick the one you're interested in and we'll get you started.

We'll be developing on Google Appengine and sharing code using Git/GitHub. If you've never used either, don't worry, we'll be starting with an introduction to both, explaining the basics and going through all the installation steps.

Bring a laptop! Or, failing that, a friend with a laptop.

From,
